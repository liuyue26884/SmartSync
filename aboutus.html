Our Core Values: Clarity, Quality, and Customer-Centricity

Clarity: Clear vision is at the heart of what we do. We believe in providing eyewear that not only enhances visual acuity but also brings clarity to our customers' lives. Transparency in our products, pricing, and services is non-negotiable. We want our customers to see the world with absolute clarity.

Quality: We're committed to delivering eyewear of the highest quality. From our handpicked materials to our meticulous craftsmanship, every pair of glasses that bears our name is a testament to our dedication to excellence. Quality assurance is not just a process; it's our promise to our customers.

Customer-Centricity: Our customers are the compass that guides our decisions. We're dedicated to understanding their unique needs and providing personalized solutions. Whether it's finding the perfect frames or ensuring a seamless shopping experience, our customers are at the forefront of everything we do. Their satisfaction is our ultimate measure of success.

Mission Statement

At TCCJY, our mission is to revolutionize the eyewear industry by providing cutting-edge, stylish, and affordable eyeglasses that not only enhance visual clarity but also reflect our customers' unique style and personality. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional quality and unmatched customer service, ensuring that every individual who wears our glasses experiences a newfound confidence in their vision and appearance.

We believe that clear vision is a fundamental right, and we strive to make it accessible to all. With a commitment to innovation and a focus on customer satisfaction, we continually push the boundaries of eyewear technology, offering a diverse range of frames and lenses to cater to every need and preference.

At TCCJY, we don't just sell glasses; we provide a transformative experience that empowers individuals to see the world with clarity, confidence, and style. Our success is measured not only by our business achievements but also by the positive impact we make on the lives of our customers and the communities we serve.

This mission statement reflects the core values and goals of a fictional glasses company called VisionCrafters. It emphasizes their commitment to quality, affordability, innovation, customer satisfaction, and community engagement. Company Background

It all started with the opening of the very first TCCJY store in Subang,Selamgor ,back in 2005.At the time, it simply called Focus Glasses and the store was nothing more than a small counter in a shopping centre.

From those humble beginnings, we have grown from a small-town optical store to a company that not only leads the eyewear retail industry in the country, but one that is set to make its mark on the region.

Following the establishment of TCCJY Sdn Bhd in 2008, a head office was set up in Subang, Selangor to provide management and operational support to the growing number of retail branches. But more opportunities beckoned, and we eventually expanded to the nation's capital, Kuala Lumpur in 2010.

At the same time, we are also embracing a knowledge-based economy where innovation and technology are essential in adding to our value, enhancing our professional services, and boosting our overall competitiveness. TCCJY is also the first in the optical market to have embarked on eCommerce with the aim of building online to offline shopping experiences for customers.
